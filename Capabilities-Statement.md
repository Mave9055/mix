### THE PROBLEM WE SOLVE

**The Gap:**
* 17.6 veterans die by suicide daily; 61% had no recent VHA contact
* Moral injury drives suicide risk but has no standard protocol
* Highest suicide rate: first year post-separation (46.2/100K)
* Clinicians are bottlenecked; veterans won't self-identify as "patients"

**Our Solution:**
* Peer-delivered curriculum that works outside clinical settings
* Session 09: Shame vs. Responsibility — direct moral injury intervention
* Transition-window psychoeducation before the nervous system fails
* Peer facilitators. No license required. Military-culture language.
