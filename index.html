Curriculum Blueprint

The 5-Part Roadmap to Healing

The "What Really Happened" curriculum is structured into five progressive parts, guiding participants from understanding the origins of Complex PTSD to developing strategies for systemic change. This modular framework ensures a comprehensive and adaptive learning journey, shifting the focus from "What is wrong with you?" to "What happened to you?"

Part I

How It Gets Built Into You

Early development and trauma formation. The nervous system in childhood.

Part II

How It Destroys Your Relationships

Adult attachment disruption, trauma bonding, and relational re-enactment.

Part III

How You Tried to Cope

Regulation strategies, addiction, the breaking point, and high-functioning presentation.

Part IV

The Internal Battlefield

Neuroscience, shame, crisis architecture, and safety protocol.

Part V

Changing the World Around You

Integration, advocacy, meaning-making, and systemic change.
